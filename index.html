Simple Line Drawing

Instructions

1. Press and hold the mouse button to start drawing

2. Move mouse to determine line direction (horizontal or vertical)

3. Continue moving in different directions to create corners automatically

4. The line will change direction when you move significantly in the perpendicular direction

5. Release the mouse button to complete the entire shape

6. Each line segment becomes a worktop with a fixed width of 600mm

7. Connected segments create connected worktops that maintain their relationship
